What is it?

You may know Guitar pro, tux guitar, Rocksmith 2014, Yousician, Rocksmith+?. Well those are cool and probably match your expectations better than RAF. I just want to have something simple (well I try), play a song, display the tablature progressively and choose the speed of the song.

How to

You can browse your files to select a guitarpro file with the song you want to learn.
Then select the instrument to display (Track selector).
Then click play.

You can download tabs on Ultimate guitar for example (you'll need an account). Then search for the song you want then filter by «Guitar Pro». Download the file (at the bottom of page).
